About Currency Wheel

Currency Wheel is a forex market dashboard for G10 major currencies. The app provides a live Currency Strength Wheel, 28-pair leaderboard, multi-timeframe confluence, strength and sentiment views, activity and volatility indicators, and optional market alerts — designed to help traders monitor currency relationships quickly and clearly.

Currency Wheel is intended for informational and educational purposes only. It does not execute trades and does not provide personalised investment advice.
